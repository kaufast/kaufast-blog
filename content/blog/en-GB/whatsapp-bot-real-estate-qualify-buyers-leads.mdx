If you're a real estate agent, you know the drill. A lead comes in through a portal — Idealista, Rightmove, Zillow. You call them back. They don't answer. You call again. They ask a few questions. They were just browsing. Multiply that by 30 leads a week and you've spent half your time on people who were never going to buy.

A **WhatsApp bot for real estate** flips this process. Instead of you chasing every enquiry, the bot engages the lead instantly, asks the right qualifying questions, matches them to relevant listings, and hands you a conversation summary with only the serious buyers. You talk to 5 qualified leads instead of chasing 30 cold ones.

## The Lead Qualification Problem

Studies show that **78% of real estate leads go to the agent who responds first**. But responding first to every portal lead is humanly impossible when you're showing a property, driving between viewings, or simply sleeping.

Most agents try to solve this with auto-responder emails. The problem? Email open rates for real estate are around 20%. By the time the lead reads your email, they've already spoken to two other agents. WhatsApp messages get opened 90%+ of the time, usually within minutes.

## How the Bot Qualifies Leads

When a new enquiry arrives, the bot starts a natural conversation on WhatsApp:

1. **Budget**: "What's your approximate budget for this purchase?"
2. **Timeline**: "When are you looking to move?"
3. **Requirements**: "How many bedrooms do you need? Any must-haves?"
4. **Finance**: "Are you pre-approved for a mortgage or paying cash?"
5. **Viewing**: "Would you like to schedule a viewing this week?"

The conversation feels natural, not like a form. Leads who are serious answer willingly. Tyre-kickers drop off after the first question — saving you from ever wasting time on them.

## Listing Matching

Beyond qualifying, the bot connects to your property database. When a buyer says they want a 3-bedroom apartment in Eixample under €400,000, the bot instantly sends matching listings with photos, floor plans, and key details. No manual searching. No delays.

When new properties match a buyer's criteria, the bot proactively sends them the listing before you've even had your morning coffee. First to respond, every time.

## Viewing Scheduling

For qualified leads who want to see a property, the bot checks your availability and books the viewing directly into your calendar. It sends the buyer the address, directions, and a confirmation. The day before, it sends a reminder. On the morning of the viewing, it confirms attendance.

Agents using this approach report **viewing no-show rates dropping from 30% to under 10%**. That's three fewer wasted trips to empty properties per week.

## Multilingual — Because Your Buyers Are International

In markets like Barcelona, the Algarve, or the Costa del Sol, buyers come from everywhere. A German couple looking for a holiday home. A British family relocating. A Mexican investor. Your bot handles all of them — in their language — simultaneously.

Our bots operate natively in 12+ languages. The German buyer messages in German, gets responses in German, and views listings with descriptions in German. You receive the summary in your language. No translator needed.

## What the Agent Gets

For each qualified lead, you receive a structured brief:
- Buyer name and WhatsApp number
- Budget range and financing status
- Property preferences (location, size, features)
- Timeline and urgency level
- Properties they've already expressed interest in
- Full conversation transcript

You pick up the phone knowing exactly who you're calling, what they want, and what they can afford. The conversion rate from these handoffs is typically **3-5x higher** than cold portal leads.

## Real Estate AI: Beyond Lead Qualification

Once the bot is running, it handles more than just new leads:

- **Seller enquiries**: Qualify homeowners who want a valuation
- **Follow-ups**: Re-engage leads who went quiet — "Hi Maria, a new property matching your criteria just listed. Would you like to see it?"
- **Market updates**: Send personalised market data to your pipeline — "Properties in your target area averaged €3,200/sqm last month, up 4%"
- **Document collection**: Request and receive documents needed for transactions

The bot doesn't replace you. It removes the administrative work that stops you from doing what you actually do best: building relationships and closing deals.
